S&P 500 Sector ETF Analysis

Analyzed 3 years of S&P 500 sector ETF data to identify defensive sectors with superior risk-adjusted returns. Built a composite scoring model using Sharpe Ratio, Beta, and Max Drawdown to rank all 11 GICS sectors. Discovered that Communication Services, Financials, and Utilities offered the best defensive characteristics during the 2023–2026 period.
